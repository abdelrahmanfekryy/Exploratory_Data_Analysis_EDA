Ford GoBike Dataset Insights¶

In This Presentation We Are Going To Cover Two Main Points:

  • The Detailed Relationship Between Member Gender And Trip Duration.
  • The Detailed Relationship Between Member User Type And Trip Duration.

Who Takes Longer Rides Customers or Subscribers?¶

In [2]:
box_plot(df,'user_type','duration_sec',ylim=(0,2500),showfliers=False)

we can see that customers have longer rides on average.¶

the next plot convey the same insight from different perspective¶

In [3]:
plt_hist(df['user_type'],df['duration_sec'],bins=2000,scale='linear',xlim=(61,4000),histtype='step')

Now That We Know That Customers Have Longer Rides ...¶

Is That Longer Rides Are Distributed Equally Through All Days ?¶

In [4]:
order = ['Saturday', 'Sunday','Monday','Tuesday','Wednesday','Thursday','Friday']
box_plot_multi(df,'start_weekday','duration_sec','user_type',ylim=(0,3500),showfliers=False,order=order)

we can see that Customers longer rides are more concentrated on weekend¶

Is That Longer Rides Are Distributed Equally Through All Day Hours ?¶

In [5]:
box_plot_multi(df,'start_hour','duration_sec','user_type',ylim=(0,3500),showfliers=False)

we can see that Customers longer rides are concentrated during business hours and at the early hours of the day¶
